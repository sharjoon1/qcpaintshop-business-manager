Privacy Policy

QC Manager - Quality Colours Paint Shop

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Quality Colours Paint Shop ("we", "us", "our") operates the QC Manager application (the "App") for internal business management. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the App:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, employee ID, and role/designation as provided during registration or by an administrator.
  • Attendance Data: Clock-in and clock-out times, including photographs taken at the time of attendance.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates at the time of clock-in and clock-out, used to verify attendance at authorized work locations.
  • Camera Images: Photos captured through the app for attendance verification and document uploads.
  • Business Data: Estimates, customer information, product details, leads, tasks, and other business-related data entered into the system.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Attendance Tracking: Photos and location data are used to verify employee attendance at designated work locations.
  • Business Operations: Customer data, estimates, and product information are used for day-to-day business operations.
  • Salary Processing: Attendance records are used for calculating salaries, overtime, and leave management.
  • Communication: Contact information may be used for work-related communication.
  • App Improvement: Usage patterns help us improve the application's functionality.

3. Camera and Location Permissions

Camera: The App requests camera access solely for capturing attendance selfies during clock-in/clock-out and for uploading business documents. Photos are uploaded to our secure servers and are not shared with third parties.

Location: The App requests location access only during clock-in and clock-out to verify your presence at an authorized work location. Location is captured as a single point-in-time reading and is not continuously tracked.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption.
  • Data is stored on secure servers with access controls.
  • Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by logging out or clearing app data.
  • Attendance photos are stored on our servers and accessible only to authorized administrators.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared only:

  • With authorized administrators and managers within Quality Colours Paint Shop for business operations.
  • When required by law or legal proceedings.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our business or employees.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Attendance records and salary data are retained as per applicable labor law requirements. You may request deletion of your account by contacting your administrator.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data stored in the system.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for camera or location access through your device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by employees aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
